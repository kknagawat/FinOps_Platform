# Refund Policy

## Overview
This document outlines the refund policy for all subscription plans offered by FinOps Analytics Platform.

## General Refund Rules

### Monthly Subscriptions
- Customers on monthly billing cycles are eligible for a full refund if requested within **7 calendar days** of the billing date.
- After 7 days, a prorated refund may be issued at management discretion based on usage during the period.
- No refunds are issued for months where the customer has consumed more than 50% of their plan's feature limits.

### Annual Subscriptions
- Full refund available within **30 calendar days** of the annual billing date.
- After 30 days but within 90 days, a prorated refund minus a **15% early termination fee** is available.
- After 90 days, no refund is available. The customer may downgrade to a lower tier for the remainder of the term.

### Enterprise Plans
- Enterprise customers have custom refund terms as specified in their Master Service Agreement (MSA).
- Default enterprise refund window is **45 calendar days** unless otherwise negotiated.
- Enterprise refunds require VP-level approval and a documented reason code.
- Enterprise customers are eligible for **service credits** in lieu of cash refunds, credited at 1.25x the refund amount.

## Exceptions
- Billing errors (duplicate charges, incorrect amounts) are always fully refundable regardless of timing.
- Service outage refunds follow the SLA credit schedule (see SLA Policy).
- Fraudulent transactions are handled by the Security team and are outside this policy.

## Refund Processing
- Refunds are processed within **5-10 business days** of approval.
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method.
- Bank transfer refunds may take up to **15 business days**.
- Cryptocurrency payments are refunded at the USD-equivalent value at the time of the original transaction.

## Escalation Path
1. Level 1: Customer Support Agent (up to $500)
2. Level 2: Support Manager (up to $5,000)
3. Level 3: VP of Customer Success (up to $50,000)
4. Level 4: CFO approval (above $50,000)
