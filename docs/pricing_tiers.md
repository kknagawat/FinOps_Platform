## Upgrade and Downgrade Rules

### Upgrades
- Upgrades take effect immediately.
- The customer is charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the current billing cycle.
- All usage limits are immediately updated to the new plan.
- Historical data retention expands to the new plan's limits retroactively.

### Downgrades
- Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
- The customer retains access to current plan features until the cycle ends.
- Data exceeding the new plan's retention limits is archived (not deleted) for 90 days.
- After 90 days, archived data is permanently deleted.
- Downgrade from annual to monthly billing incurs a **10% conversion fee** on the remaining balance.

### Plan-Specific Notes

#### Free Plan
- Limited to 1,000 API calls per month.
- No phone or chat support.
- Data retained for 7 days only.
- Cannot be combined with add-ons.

#### Enterprise Plan
- Minimum 12-month commitment.
- Custom pricing based on volume, users, and integration requirements.
- Includes quarterly business reviews (QBRs) with Customer Success.
- Custom SLA terms available (up to 99.99% with dedicated infrastructure).
- Volume discounts: 10% for 2-year commitment, 20% for 3-year commitment.
